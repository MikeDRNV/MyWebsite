5 OF THE WORLD'S MOST BEAUTIFUL CARS

"Beautiful" is a tough word to use when it comes to car design. There are many new cars that are great looking, striking, interesting, or thought-provoking, but it's difficult to make the jump to beautiful. The bar is high, and the criteria for clearing it is subjective-which is to say, the 5 cars i've gathered here are the most beautiful cars. Read on to see the full roundup, and feel free to yell at your screen about how wrong you think we are. Design, after all, is subjective.

5. alfa romeo disco volante Touring Superleggera

For the first time about the car was known in 2012, and already in 2013 was presented at the Geneva Motor Show. The car is assembled exclusively by order and for the construction you need the existing Alfa Romeo 8C Competizione. Milan atelier does not disclose the cost of construction. For the manufacture of the body using aluminum and carbon fiber, in total for manual work takes more than 4000 hours. Compared to the 8C Competizione, the car has become lower, wider and longer. The engine is the same 4.7 liter V8, but with a modified exhaust system, the suspension has become softer, and the style of the interior is negotiated with the client. In 2013, Alfa Romeo Disco Volante received the prestigious Concorso d'Eleganza Villa d'Este Award as the most beautiful prototype.

4. Jaguar XKSS

The stunning XKSS, finished in Sherwood Green paint, has been created by the Jaguar Classic engineering team ahead of the production of nine cars for delivery to customers across the globe in 2017.
Often referred to as the world's first supercar, the XKSS was originally made by Jaguar as a road-going conversion of the Le Mans-winning D-type, which was built from 1954-1956. In 1957, nine cars earmarked for export to North America were lost in a fire at Jaguar's Browns Lane factory in the British Midlands.
Earlier this year Jaguar announced that its Classic division would build the nine 'lost' XKSS sports cars for a select group of established collectors and customers. The new one-off XKSS presented in Los Angeles is the summation of 18 months of research and will be used as a blueprint from which the nine continuation cars are built.
The nine cars will be completely new, with period chassis numbers from the XKSS chassis log. All cars are now sold at prices in excess of pound sterling 1 million each.

3. ferrari f430 scuderia

Ferrari F430 Scuderia - the anniversary road version of the serial sports car, released by the legendary company from Maranello - Ferrari. In September 2007, at the Frankfurt Motor Show F430, Scuderia was represented by seven-time Formula 1 racing champion Michael Schumacher.
The track car is adapted for expensive public use. F430 Scuderia is equipped with a stabilization control system, it includes five modes.
The curb weight of the sports car has reached 1250 kg. Its dry weight is 100 kg less compared to the Ferrari F430 coupe. Thanks to this, the F430 Scuderia is one of the most powerful energy weapons.
The V8 engine is boosted to 510 liters. from. An advanced 6-speed robotic semi-automatic gearbox allows you to shift gears at a speed of 60 milliseconds.
The car's interior is made in a traditionally aggressive sports style (carbon, carbon fiber).

2. Koenigsegg Agera

Agera means 'to take action' in Swedish. It is also short for the ancient Greek word Ageratos which means 'ageless'. These are both suitable descriptors for this incredible and beautiful machine.

Koenigsegg came up with a new interior, featuring the now famous 'ghost' lighting system, where all interior symbols are created with laser etched, virtually invisible nano-holes through the aluminum panels and buttons.

Furthermore, the Agera featured a unique 7 speed transmission with a E-diff and wet clutch functionality developed by Koenigsegg, a transmission that truly enhanced performance and handling.

The Agera was the first model to feature the Koenigsegg developed VGR wheels. They are real air turbines, increasing the downforce of the car by measurable amounts and improving brake cooling. All four wheels are individual so that turbine blades always face the correct direction for extraction. Given that the offset and width is different front to rear, all four wheels have their own unique design. The VGR wheels are forged and then fully machined to the final shape. All excess material is removed during the forging and machining process, thus minimizing weight whilst displaying outstanding levels of stiffness.

The Agera Became the 2010 Top Gear Hypercar of the Year.

1. maclaren p1

Designed, engineered and built to be the best driver's car in the world.
The McLaren P1 is an English hybrid car with a hybrid engine, the successor to the McLaren F1, manufactured since 2013 by McLaren Automotive. At the Geneva Motor Show in March 2013, the world premiere of the final version of the car took place. A total of 375 cars will be produced at a price of about a million euros. For maximum weight reduction, the interior of the P1 is soundproofed and made of carbon fiber. The seats are located as close as possible to each other for a greater concentration of the mass, the adjustment of their location is set in advance and subsequently can only be changed by the dealer. The driver receives information from three TFT displays - one 6.8-inch in the middle and 2 smaller ones slightly behind. Of the security systems, the car has electronic stability control and traction control system.
Unlike its predecessor, the P1 has 2 seats, not three, and it accelerates to 300 km / h 5 seconds faster; The concept of the car almost did not differ from the serial version. LED headlights of the car are made in the form of a company icon. In addition, the car has a small trunk in front.
